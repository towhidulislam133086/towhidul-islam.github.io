--More about me--

Myself, Towhidul Islam. I have completed my B.Sc (engr.) degree in 2018 from the department of Computer Science & Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), one of the best public engineering universities in Bangladesh. Since my graduation, I have been working as a Lecturer in CSE at different private universities in Bangladesh. Currently I am affiliated with Northern University Bangladesh (NUB). My primary research interests are in Applying Deep Learning to Medical Imaging, AI for Health, Bioinformatics, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning.
During my undergraduate thesis, I worked on Cancer Disease Prediction under the supervision of Mahit Kumar Paul sir, Assistant Professor, Dept. of CSE, RUET and A H M Sarowar Sattar sir, Professor, Dept. of CSE RUET.

Apart from this, I am also a self motivated and active researcher. For example, recently, I have initiated collaborative research projects on Monkeypox Detection using AI and Surveys on AI for X-ray, CT, and Ultra-sound based COVID-19 Detection with Dr. Mohammad Arafat Hussain, a research fellow at Harvard Medical School, affiliated with Bostons' Children Hospital. To successfully finish the projects different groups have been made and I am the team leader of one of the groups.

Currently, I am actively looking for available MSc or PhD position which aligns my research interests.
